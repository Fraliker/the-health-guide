# Nervous system problems

## Symptoms of peripheral neuropathy
- Balance impairments;
- Blood pressure abnormalities;
- Burning or tingling;
- Muscle cramps;
- Muscle twitching;
- Muscle weakness;
- Numbness;
- Pain;
- Pulse abnormalities;
- Sensitivity to touch;
- Walking difficulties.
